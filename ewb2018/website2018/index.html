Making friends in Mkutani, Tanzania

In January 2018, two of our members got the opportunity to travel to Tanzania to conduct an assessment trip and learn about the community's needs.

Goal: Clean Drinking Water

This is what the nearest river to Mkutani looks like. Current filtration techniques aren't enough to rid the water of harmful bacteria, so another source must be utilized.
